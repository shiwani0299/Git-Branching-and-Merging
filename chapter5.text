A dominant alien species, capable of traveling between galaxies and manipulating reality, sets a plan in motion to conquer the infinite universe. With their advanced technology, they penetrate the metaverse, transforming it into a weapon for controlling civilizations across the cosmos. Their objective is to dissolve the boundaries between the digital and physical worlds, bringing both under their command. As their influence extends through the galaxies, a small group of humans discovers the alien scheme, though their attempts to stop it seem insignificant against the aliens' overwhelming and relentless expansion.
